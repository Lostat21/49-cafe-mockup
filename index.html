Heritage & Vision

Our Story

Founded in 2018 by literary scholar Eleanor Ashworth, 49 Cafe was born from a desire to merge the quiet dignity of a private library with the warmth of a European café. Housed in a Georgian townhouse on Great Russell Street, we've become a beloved sanctuary for writers, academics, and dreamers.

Every detail — from the hand-bound menus to the rare tea collection — reflects our commitment to craftsmanship and slow living.
